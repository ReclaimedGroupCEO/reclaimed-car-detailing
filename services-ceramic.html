What Is Covenant Coatings™?

More than a service name — it's a promise. Every Covenant Coating installation comes with our full commitment to preparation, application, and aftercare. We don't cut corners on something this permanent.

9H

9H Ceramic Hardness

Professional-grade SiO2 ceramic that's harder than your paint — protecting against minor swirls, UV, and chemical contamination.

H2O

Hydrophobic Self-Cleaning

Water beads and rolls off, taking dirt with it. Your car stays cleaner longer between washes — noticeably different from the first rain.

UV

UV & Oxidation Shield

Alabama sun is harsh. Ceramic blocks UV rays that cause paint to fade, oxidize, and lose gloss over time.

CHM

Chemical Resistance

Resistant to bird droppings, tree sap, road salt, and harsh cleaners that would otherwise etch or stain bare paint.

GLS

Enhanced Gloss

Ceramic adds depth and reflectivity beyond what wax or sealant can achieve — a wet, glass-like finish that lasts years.

WRT

Written Warranty

Every Covenant Coating comes with a written warranty covering premature failure, de-lamination, and loss of hydrophobic effect.

Our Process

How We Install Covenant Coatings™

Ceramic coating is only as good as the preparation beneath it. Our process is methodical — no shortcuts, no skipped steps.

1

Full Decontamination

Iron fallout, clay bar, and tar removal to strip every bonded contaminant before we polish.

2

Paint Correction

Machine polish to remove swirls and defects — because ceramic locks in what's there, good or bad.

3

Panel Wipedown

IPA wipedown removes all oils and polish residue so the coating bonds directly to bare clear coat.

4

Ceramic Application

Panel-by-panel ceramic application in a controlled environment, leveled and left to flash properly.

5

Cure & Inspect

Coating is inspected under specialist lighting for high spots or missed areas, then allowed to cure.
