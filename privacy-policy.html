Privacy Policy

How Reclaimed Detailers LLC collects, uses, and protects your information.

Last updated: January 2026

Reclaimed Detailers LLC ("we," "us," or "our") respects your privacy. This policy explains what information we collect when you use our website or services, and how we handle it.

Information we collect

We only collect information you choose to give us — typically your name, phone number, email address, and details about your vehicle and location when you submit a booking or contact request. We may also collect basic, non-identifying analytics about how visitors use our site.

How we use it

  • To respond to your inquiries and confirm bookings
  • To provide and improve our detailing services
  • To send updates, specials, or community news — only if you opt in

Sharing

We do not sell, rent, or trade your personal information. We share it only with trusted service providers who help us operate (such as scheduling or email tools), and only as needed to serve you.

Cookies

Our site may use simple cookies to remember your preferences and understand site usage. You can disable cookies in your browser at any time.

Your choices

You can ask us to access, correct, or delete the information we hold about you at any time. To do so, contact us at reclaimeddetailers@outlook.com or (334) 640-1831.

Contact

Questions about this policy? Reach us anytime at reclaimeddetailers@outlook.com.
