01 Introduction

Reclaimed Detailers LLC ("Company," "we," "us," or "our") operates this website located at reclaimeddetailers.com (the "Site"). This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and protect information about you when you visit our Site or use our services.

By using this Site, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this Privacy Policy. If you do not agree with the terms of this policy, please do not access the Site.

02 Information We Collect

2.1 Contact & Booking Data

When you submit a contact or booking form on our Site, we collect the following information you voluntarily provide:

  • Full name
  • Email address
  • Phone number
  • Message content

Form submissions are transmitted via FormSubmit (formsubmit.co), a third-party form delivery service, and delivered directly to our email address. This data is not stored on our servers.

2.2 Membership Account Data

Our membership portal allows you to set up a local account. The following data may be stored in your browser's localStorage:

  • Name and email identifier
  • Subscription tier (e.g., Basic, Standard, Elite)
  • Ceramic coating verification details (for Elite tier eligibility)

This data is stored client-side only — it exists solely in your own browser and is never transmitted to or stored on our servers.

2.3 Preferences

Your browser's localStorage is also used to store your theme preference (light or dark mode). We do not use server-side analytics, tracking cookies, or third-party advertising cookies on this Site.

03 How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect for the following purposes:

  • To respond to your inquiries and contact form submissions
  • To schedule and confirm detailing service appointments
  • To verify Elite membership tier eligibility (ceramic coating verification)
  • To improve and personalize your website experience
  • To communicate important updates about your service or account

04 Information Sharing

We do not sell, trade, rent, or otherwise transfer your personal information to outside parties for commercial purposes. We may share limited information only in the following circumstances:

  • FormSubmit: Your contact form submissions are processed through FormSubmit's delivery service solely to route messages to our email inbox.
  • Legal requirements: We may disclose information if required to do so by law, or in response to valid requests by public authorities (e.g., a court or government agency).

05 Third-Party Links

Our Site may contain links to third-party websites and social media platforms, including:

  • Instagram
  • Facebook
  • TikTok
  • Google

We are not responsible for the privacy practices or content of those external sites. We encourage you to review the privacy policies of any third-party sites you visit.

06 Data Security

We take reasonable precautions to protect your information. Since contact form data is transmitted via FormSubmit and membership data is stored only in your browser's localStorage, there is minimal server-side data exposure.

However, no method of data transmission over the Internet or electronic storage is 100% secure. While we strive to use commercially acceptable means to protect your information, we cannot guarantee its absolute security.

07 Children's Privacy

This Site is not directed to children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personally identifiable information from children under 13. If you are a parent or guardian and believe that your child has provided us with personal information, please contact us immediately at reclaimeddetailers@outlook.com so that we can take appropriate action.

08 Your Rights

You have the following rights regarding your information:

  • Access: You may request information about the personal data we hold about you.
  • Correction: You may request that we correct inaccurate information.
  • Deletion: You may request that we delete your personal information from our records.
  • Local Data: You can remove locally stored membership and preference data at any time by clearing your browser's localStorage via your browser settings.

To exercise any of these rights, please contact us at:

reclaimeddetailers@outlook.com

(334) 640-1831

09 California Residents (CCPA)

If you are a California resident, you may have additional rights under the California Consumer Privacy Act (CCPA), including the right to know what personal information is collected, the right to know whether your personal information is sold or disclosed and to whom, the right to opt out of the sale of your personal information, and the right to non-discrimination for exercising your CCPA rights.

We do not sell personal information. To exercise any of your California privacy rights, please contact us directly at reclaimeddetailers@outlook.com.

10 Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. When we do, we will revise the "Last Updated" date at the top of this page. Your continued use of the Site following the posting of any changes constitutes your acceptance of those changes.

We encourage you to periodically review this Privacy Policy to stay informed about how we are protecting your information.
