Paint Correction

Paint correction.

Multi-stage machine polishing removes swirls, light scratches, and oxidation — restoring the paint’s original depth, gloss, and clarity.

What It Is

Defects, removed for good.

Those cobweb swirls you see under the sun, the haze, the light scratches — they live in your clear coat, and no wash will remove them. Paint correction does, permanently.

We assess your paint depth, then machine-polish in single or multiple stages to cut away the damaged layer and refine the surface to a deep, wet-looking gloss. It is also the essential prep before any ceramic coating.

What's included

  • Paint depth assessment
  • Single or multi-stage cut
  • Swirl & haze removal
  • Oxidation correction
  • Light scratch refinement
  • Gloss enhancement
  • Panel-by-panel inspection
  • Pre-coating prep
