Accessibility Statement

We want this site to work for every visitor, including those using assistive technology.

Last updated: June 2026

Reclaimed Detailers LLC is committed to making our website usable by as many people as possible, regardless of ability or the technology they use to browse the web. Accessibility is an ongoing effort, not a one-time fix, and we keep improving as we update the site.

What we've built in

  • Semantic HTML structure (headings, lists, landmarks) so screen readers can navigate the page logically.
  • Color combinations chosen to meet readable contrast in both light and dark sections.
  • Text that resizes cleanly without breaking layout, and a responsive design that works on phones, tablets, and desktops.
  • Reduced-motion support — visitors with "reduce motion" enabled in their operating system see the site with scroll animations turned off.
  • Descriptive link and button text rather than generic "click here" language.

Where we're still improving

As we add real photography and video to the site, we're committed to writing meaningful alt text for every image rather than leaving it blank or generic. If you notice a missing description, a keyboard trap, low contrast, or anything else that gets in your way, we want to know — see below.

Standard we aim for

We use the Web Content Accessibility Guidelines (WCAG) 2.1, Level AA as our reference point. We don't claim full certification — we treat it as a working target and a checklist we hold ourselves to.

Let us know

If any part of this site is difficult to use with a screen reader, keyboard, or other assistive technology, please tell us. We'll work with you to provide the information or service you need another way, and we'll prioritize fixing the underlying issue.

Contact us at reclaimeddetailers@outlook.com or (334) 640-1831. We try to respond to accessibility requests within two business days.
