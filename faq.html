Questions

Good to know.

Everything you might want to ask before booking. Still curious? We are a call or text away.

General
Do you really come to me?
Yes — we are fully mobile. We bring our own water and power and detail your vehicle at your home or workplace anywhere in the Autauga County area, including Prattville, Millbrook, Montgomery, Wetumpka, Pike Road, and Elmore County.
What are your hours?
Our hours vary by day: Monday 7am–8pm, Tuesday 5:30–8pm, Wednesday 7am–5:30pm, Friday 5:30–8pm, and Saturday 7am–7pm. We are closed Thursday and Sunday. Booking ahead secures your preferred time slot — especially for paint correction and ceramic coating work.
Are you licensed and insured?
Fully licensed and insured. Your vehicle is in careful, accountable hands from the first rinse to the final wipe-down.
Services & Process
How long does a full detail take?
Most full details take three to five hours depending on the vehicle size and condition. Paint correction and ceramic coatings are scheduled separately as they are multi-stage, multi-hour services.
What is a ceramic coating, and is it worth it?
A ceramic coating is a professional-grade protective layer that bonds to your paint, making it hydrophobic, UV-resistant, and far easier to keep clean. Our Covenant Coatings program protects your finish for years — the best long-term investment for a vehicle you intend to keep.
What is the difference between paint correction and a wash?
A wash cleans the surface; paint correction removes defects from within the clear coat — swirls, light scratches, and oxidation — using multi-stage machine polishing to restore depth and gloss. It is the prep we recommend before a ceramic coating.
Booking & Payment
How do memberships work?
Choose Bronze, Silver, or Gold and we keep your vehicle on a recurring schedule. No contracts, cancel anytime, and members receive priority booking and member-only pricing on additional services.
What do you need from me on the day?
Access to the vehicle and a bit of space to work. We bring our own water and power — you do not need to provide anything. We confirm the details when we lock in your booking.
What if I am not satisfied?
We do not leave until the work meets our standard. If something is not right, we fix it before we go — that is our 100% satisfaction guarantee.
