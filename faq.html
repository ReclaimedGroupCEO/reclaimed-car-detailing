Services & Results
A basic wash cleans the surface. A full detail is a comprehensive restoration — decontamination, clay bar treatment, paint correction (if needed), interior deep clean, conditioning, and protection applied. A detail can easily take 3–4 hours per vehicle. It's a completely different level of care.
It depends on the service and vehicle condition. A maintenance wash takes about 1 hour. An exterior detail runs 1.5–2 hours. A full interior and exterior detail typically takes 3–4 hours. Paint correction can take a full day depending on the severity. We always give you a realistic time estimate when booking.
Paint correction removes defects in the clear coat — swirl marks, light scratches, water spots, and oxidation. Deep scratches that have penetrated to the base coat or primer cannot be removed by polishing alone and may require touch-up paint or bodywork. We'll assess your paint and give you an honest assessment before we start.

Ceramic Coating
A professionally applied ceramic coating typically lasts 2–5 years with proper maintenance. The longevity depends on how often the vehicle is washed, what products are used, and environmental exposure. Using pH-neutral soaps and regular maintenance washes significantly extends the coating's life.
Ceramic coating is low-maintenance but not no-maintenance. We recommend: washing every 4–6 weeks with pH-neutral soap, avoiding automatic drive-through washes (the harsh chemicals and brushes can degrade the coating), and scheduling a professional ceramic maintenance wash every 6–12 months. Our Gold membership includes quarterly ceramic maintenance.
In most cases, yes. Ceramic coating seals whatever is underneath it — including defects. Applying coating over scratches and swirls locks them in permanently. We recommend at minimum a single-stage paint correction before coating to ensure the finish looks its best under the ceramic. We'll assess your paint and advise during the consultation.
