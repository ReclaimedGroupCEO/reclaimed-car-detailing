Membership FAQ

Your membership bills monthly on the date you first sign up. There are no contracts or commitments — you can cancel anytime with 7 days notice before your next billing date.
Life happens. You can pause your membership once per year without penalty. Just reach out to Ray at least 7 days before your next billing date and we'll hold your spot.
Visits do not roll over month to month. We design the membership so visits are spaced to maintain your vehicle optimally — skipping too long between services means more work is needed next time.
Absolutely. You can change plans at any time and the new pricing takes effect at your next billing cycle. Upgrades are applied immediately, so you get the better service right away.
Each membership covers one vehicle. If you have multiple vehicles, reach out to discuss a custom bundle — we can work something out for households with 2+ vehicles.
We stand behind every job. If something doesn't meet your standards, call Ray directly and we'll make it right — no questions asked. Your satisfaction is our reputation.
