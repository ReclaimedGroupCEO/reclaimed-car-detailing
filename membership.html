Elite Plan — Ceramic Coating Requirement

The Elite membership is designed to maintain and protect ceramic-coated vehicles. To qualify, your vehicle must have an existing ceramic coating applied by Reclaimed Detailers or another reputable detailing shop. When you select the Elite plan, you'll be asked to provide the shop name and approximate date of application. We may verify this before activating your plan.

Questions & Answers

Frequently Asked Questions

How does the mobile detailing service work?

We come directly to your home, workplace, or any preferred location. Once you're a member, you schedule each detail via phone, text, or email. Our team arrives with all equipment and supplies — you don't need to do a thing.

Can I roll over unused details to the next month?

Details do not roll over between billing months. We encourage members to schedule early in the month to make the most of their plan. If you consistently can't use all your details, we'd recommend stepping down to a lower tier.

What if I need to cancel or change my plan?

You can cancel or change your plan any time through your account dashboard. Changes take effect at the start of your next billing cycle. There are no cancellation fees or long-term contracts.

Does my vehicle need a ceramic coating to join the Elite plan?

Yes — the Elite plan ($225/month) is specifically designed for ceramic-coated vehicles. The maintenance services included are formulated to protect and extend the life of professional ceramic coatings. If your vehicle doesn't have a coating yet, ask us about our Ceramic Coating service as a one-time add-on before enrolling in Elite.

Do you serve the entire Montgomery, AL area?

Yes, we serve Montgomery and surrounding areas. Contact us at (334) 640-1831 to confirm service availability at your specific address. We're always expanding our coverage area.
