Our Story

Faith first.
Community always.

This was never just a detailing business. It's a calling — and every vehicle we touch reflects that commitment.

Ray ComptonFounder · Reclaimed Detailers LLC
Built on Integrity

Driven by craft.

Reclaimed Detailers began not with a business plan, but with a belief: that every person's vehicle deserves the same care and respect they'd give their own. Ray Compton founded this company in Autauga County, Alabama, with a passion for detail work and a deep conviction that doing good work for people is a form of service.

What started as a side operation quickly grew into a full-time calling. Ray brings the same intentionality to every job that he brings to every relationship — no shortcuts, no "good enough," no leaving until the work is done right. That has built something rare in this industry: genuine trust.

Today we serve families, professionals, and enthusiasts throughout Autauga County and beyond. But the mission hasn't changed. It's still about doing excellent work, honoring people with your best effort, and giving back to the community that made us.

"Whatever you do, work at it with all your heart, as working for the Lord, not for human masters." Colossians 3:23 — the verse behind every job we do

What We Stand For

Our core values.

✚

Faith Before Everything

Every job is an act of service. We approach our work the way we approach our faith — with humility, dedication, and the understanding that how you do anything is how you do everything.

★

Excellence, Not Excuses

We don't rationalize subpar work. If it doesn't meet our standard, we fix it — no charge. Excellence isn't a selling point; it's a non-negotiable.

☼

Servant's Heart

We're here to serve you — showing up on time, communicating clearly, and going beyond what was asked. Your satisfaction is our measure of success.

✓

Restoration Focused

There's something powerful about restoring something to its original condition. Vehicles — like people — deserve a chance to look and feel their best again.

☖

Community Rooted

Autauga County made us. 20% of every transaction goes back into the community through local charitable giving. This isn't marketing — it's a commitment on every invoice.

◯

Transparency Always
