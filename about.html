Ray ComptonFounder · Aioneo Auto Detailing
Built on Integrity

Driven by craft.

Ray Compton started Aioneo Auto Detailing at 17 years old right here in Elmore County, Alabama. Not as a summer job — as a real business, built from scratch, driven by faith and a genuine passion for doing excellent work on people's vehicles.

The name “Reclaimed” means something. There's a before and after to restoration — a vehicle that looked worn, neglected, or just tired, brought back to something it can be proud of. Ray believes people deserve that same care, and that running a business is one of the most meaningful ways to serve your community when you do it right.

At 17, Ray brings the same seriousness to every job that most people twice his age are still working toward — no shortcuts, no half-finished work, no leaving before the customer is happy. It is what it is: someone who cares, showing up and doing the work.

“Whatever you do, work at it with all your heart, as working for the Lord, not for human masters.” Colossians 3:23 — the verse behind every job we do

What We Stand For

Our core values.

✚

Faith Before Everything

Every job is an act of service. We approach our work the way we approach our faith — with humility, dedication, and the understanding that how you do anything is how you do everything.

★

Excellence, Not Excuses

We don't rationalize subpar work. If it doesn't meet our standard, we fix it — no charge. Excellence isn't a selling point; it's a non-negotiable.

☼

Servant's Heart

We're here to serve you — showing up on time, communicating clearly, and going beyond what was asked. Your satisfaction is our measure of success.

✓

Restoration Focused

There's something powerful about restoring something to its original condition. Vehicles — like people — deserve a chance to look and feel their best again.

☖

Community Rooted

Elmore County made us. 20% of every transaction goes back into the community through local charitable giving. This isn't marketing — it's a commitment on every invoice.

◯

Transparency Always

You'll always know what we're doing, why, and what it costs before we start. No surprises, no upsells you didn't ask for, no smoke and mirrors.
