Our Story

Built on Integrity.
Driven by Craft.

Reclaimed Detailers began not with a business plan, but with a belief: that every person's vehicle deserves to be treated with the same care and respect they'd give their own. Ray Compton founded this company in Montgomery, Alabama, with nothing more than a passion for detail work and a deep conviction that doing good work for people is a form of service.

What started as a side operation quickly grew into a full-time calling. Ray brings the same intentionality to every job that he brings to every relationship — no shortcuts, no "good enough," no leaving until the work is done right. That reputation has built something rare in the detailing industry: genuine trust.

Today, Reclaimed Detailers serves families, professionals, and enthusiasts throughout the Montgomery metro area. But the mission hasn't changed. It's still about doing excellent work, honoring people with your best effort, and giving back to the community that made you.

What We Stand For

Our Core Values

✝
Faith Before Everything

Every job is an act of service. We approach our work the same way we approach our faith — with humility, dedication, and the understanding that how you do anything is how you do everything.

🏆
Excellence, Not Excuses

We don't rationalize subpar work. If it doesn't meet our standard, we fix it — no charge. Excellence isn't a selling point; it's a non-negotiable.

🤲
Servant's Heart

We're here to serve you. That means showing up on time, communicating clearly, and going beyond what was asked. Your satisfaction is our measure of success.

🔄
Restoration Focused

There's something powerful about restoring something to its original condition. We believe vehicles — like people — deserve a chance to look and feel their best again.

🏘️
Community Rooted

Montgomery made us. 20% of every transaction goes back into the community through local charitable giving. This isn't marketing — it's a commitment we make on every invoice.

🔍
Transparency Always

You'll always know what we're doing, why we're doing it, and what it costs before we start. No surprises, no upsells you didn't ask for, no smoke and mirrors.
