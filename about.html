Who We Are

Built on Integrity.
Driven by Craft.

Reclaimed Detailers was founded with a single conviction: that excellent work, done with honesty and humility, is an act of worship. We are a Christian-owned mobile detailing company based in Montgomery, Alabama, and we put God first in every decision we make — from the products we use to the way we treat every customer who trusts us with their vehicle.

We didn’t start this business to be the biggest. We started it to be the most trustworthy. We believe that when you book with us, you’re not just getting a clean car — you’re entering into a relationship built on the same values we hold in our daily lives: integrity, craftsmanship, and genuine care for people.

“Whatever you do, work heartily, as for the Lord and not for men.”
— Colossians 3:23

Whether we’re doing a routine maintenance wash or a full restoration on a neglected vehicle, we approach every job the same way — with the standard of excellence that reflects who we work for. That’s not a marketing line. That’s the foundation of everything we do.

What We Stand For

Our Core Values

These aren’t just words on a page — they’re the principles we return to every single day, with every single vehicle we touch.

Faith Before Everything

We are a Christian-owned business and we make no apology for it. Our faith shapes how we treat customers, employees, and our community. We pray over our work, lead with integrity, and aim to reflect something greater than ourselves in every detail we deliver.

Excellence, Not Excuses

We don’t compete on price — we compete on quality and trust. Premium products, thorough technique, and a standard of work we’re willing to stand behind publicly. If it’s not right, we make it right. No shortcuts. No compromises.

Servant’s Heart

We genuinely love what we do and who we do it for. We show up on time, communicate clearly, document our work, and follow up after every appointment. Treating customers the way we’d want to be treated isn’t a policy — it’s a personal commitment.

Restoration Focused

Our name says it all. We are in the business of reclaiming things — vehicles, pride of ownership, and peace of mind. There’s something deeply satisfying about taking something worn down and bringing it back to life. That’s what drives us every day.

Community Rooted

Montgomery is home. We’re not a franchise or a faceless operation — we’re neighbors. We invest in local events, support families in our community, and give 20% of every transaction to charitable causes because a thriving business should build a thriving community.

Transparency Always

No hidden fees, no inflated quotes, no vague promises. We tell you exactly what we’re doing, why we’re doing it, and what results to expect. Honesty isn’t a competitive advantage to us — it’s just the right way to do business.

Giving Back

Business With a Purpose

We believe that every dollar earned is an opportunity to do good. That’s why we’ve built generosity into the foundation of Reclaimed Detailers — not as an afterthought, but as a core part of how we operate.

20% of every transaction goes directly to charitable causes. That means when you book a detail with us, you’re not just investing in your vehicle — you’re investing in families, organizations, and communities that need it most.

  • 20%
    Charitable Giving on Every Transaction

    A portion of every booking goes to vetted charitable causes in and around our community.

  • Funding Local Car Events

    We sponsor and participate in local car shows and automotive community events in the Montgomery area.

  • Holiday Community Events

    Around the holidays, we fund and participate in local events giving families a safe, joyful celebration.

  • ✝
    Faith-Motivated Generosity

    Our giving isn’t about publicity — it’s about purpose. We give because we believe it’s the right thing to do, full stop.
