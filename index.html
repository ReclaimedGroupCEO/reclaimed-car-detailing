Questions

Good to know.

Do you really come to me?
Yes — we're fully mobile. We bring our own water and power and detail your vehicle at your home or workplace anywhere in the Autauga County area, including Prattville, Millbrook, Montgomery, Wetumpka, and Pike Road.
How long does a full detail take?
Most full details take three to five hours depending on the vehicle's size and condition. Paint correction and ceramic coatings are scheduled separately as they're multi-stage, multi-hour services.
What is a ceramic coating, and is it worth it?
A ceramic coating is a professional-grade protective layer that bonds to your paint, making it hydrophobic, UV-resistant, and far easier to keep clean. Our Covenant Coatings program protects your finish for years — it's the best long-term investment for a vehicle you intend to keep.
How do memberships work?
Choose Bronze, Silver, or Gold and we keep your vehicle on a recurring schedule. No contracts, cancel anytime, and members receive priority booking and member-only pricing on additional services.
Are you insured?
Fully licensed and insured. Your vehicle is in careful, accountable hands from the first rinse to the final wipe-down.
