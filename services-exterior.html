More Than a Car Wash

A standard car wash removes loose dirt from the surface. Our exterior detailing service goes deeper — removing bonded contaminants that regular washing can't touch. Iron fallout from brake dust embeds in your clear coat. Industrial fallout bonds to paint. Road tar sticks to panels. These contaminants don't just look bad — over time, they cause real damage.

We use a two-bucket hand wash method, pH-neutral shampoo, and dedicated clay bar treatment to safely decontaminate every painted surface. The result isn't just clean — it's a surface that's truly prepared, whether for protection or just for the pride of a car that looks right.

Because we're fully mobile, we bring everything we need directly to you. No appointments at a shop, no driving around with a dirty car, no waiting in line.

Exterior Detailing FAQ

A standard exterior wash and decontamination takes 1.5 to 2.5 hours depending on vehicle size and condition. We never rush — the process is done right or not at all.

Just have your vehicle parked in an accessible spot with enough room for us to work around it. We handle everything else. If possible, a shaded area is preferred but not required.

A wash removes loose surface dirt. Decontamination removes bonded contaminants — iron particles, industrial fallout, road tar — that a regular wash physically cannot lift. True decontamination is what prevents long-term paint damage.

For most drivers, a full exterior detail every 4–6 weeks is ideal. If your vehicle is coated (ceramic or wax), a maintenance wash every 2–3 weeks helps preserve that protection. Our membership plans are designed around exactly this.
